OUR STRATEGY

Our business model is focused on analysing and identifying opportunities in the online retail market in South Africa and abroad. We source, import, sell and distribute products to this market through e-commerce channels. We either source these products and goods locally or import from trusted international manufacturers and wholesalers. Our approach leverages analysing e-commerce trends and actively testing the market in order to determine where opportunities lie.
