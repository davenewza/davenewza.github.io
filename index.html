ABOUT US

Tradeworks Online (Pty) Ltd. is a generalist import and distribution business based in Cape Town, South Africa. Our business model is focused on analysing and identifying opportunities in the online retail market in South Africa. We source, import (where necessary), and locally distribute products to this market through e-commerce channels. We either source products and goods locally or import from international manufacturers and wholesalers. Our approach leverages analytical models, e-commerce trends and actively testing the market in order to determine opportunities.
